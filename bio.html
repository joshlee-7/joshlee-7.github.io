About Me

Hello there! My name is Josh Lee and I am a senior at Northwestern University studying computer science. I am naturally curious and love working with others to create and build solutions to complex problems.

Previously, I have assisted Prof. Hartline at Northwestern to develop a peer grading web application that has been used by over 200 students. Most recently, I interned at the Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago as a software engineering intern for Summer 2022 where I worked on internal tools projects.

I am always looking to expand my knowledge in the different fields of computer science and am passionate about developing technology for the future!

In my free time, I like to play basketball and practice playing my bass guitar. My favorite teams are the LA Lakers and Dodgers as well as the Northwestern Wildcats. Go Cats!
